Worksheets

Worksheet 1

Plants

  1. Clover
    1. Head of many small white or pink flowers at top of stem
    2. Short
    3. Leaves in 3 leaflets
  2. Daisy family
    1. Rosette at bottom of plant
    2. Flower head with lots of tiny flowers
    3. White or yellow
  3. Buttercup
    1. Five shiny yellow petals
    2. Symmetrical flower
    3. Flowers on stalks
  4. Nettle
    1. Stinging hairs
    2. Tall
    3. Toothed leaves in opposite pairs
  5. Grass
    1. Long, flat, narrow leaf that surrounds stem
    2. No petals
    3. Bumpy nodes along stem

Invertebrates

  1. Butterflies & moths
    1. Four scaly wings
    2. Long antenna
    3. Long slim body
  2. Bees
    1. Stripey
    2. Big tongue
    3. Long antenna
  3. Snails & slugs
    1. Long slimy body with no legs
    2. Long antenna
    3. Snails have shell on back
  4. Worms
    1. Long, thin, cylindrical body
    2. No legs or eyes
    3. Wiggly!
  5. Spiders
    1. Eight legs
    2. Lots of eyes
    3. They have a waist!

Worksheet 2

Plants

  1. Thistle
    1. Spiny
    2. Tall
    3. Pink or purple flower heads with lots of tiny flowers
  2. Rose or Bramble
    1. Five petals
    2. Leaves with five leaflets
    3. Large and spiny
  3. Dock
    1. Strong vein underneath leaf in middle
    2. Tongue-shaped leaf
    3. Seeds in dense spike at top of plant
  4. Plantain
    1. Long, straight veins on leaf
    2. All leaves in rosette at base of plant
    3. Tiny flowers packed into dense head on top of stalk
  5. Carrot
    1. Tiny white flowers
    2. Umbrella shaped flower head
    3. Large, divided leaf

Invertebrates

  1. Hoverflies & other flies
    1. Big eyes
    2. Short antenna
    3. Short tongue
  2. Grasshoppers & crickets
    1. Long antenna
    2. Big back legs for jumping
    3. Big mouth parts
  3. Beetles
    1. Six legs
    2. Large shield-like back
    3. Line down middle of back where wings separate
  4. Woodlice
    1. 14 legs
    2. Small, segmented body
    3. Grey/brown in colour
  5. Shield bugs
    1. Flat, broad (shield-like) bodies with six legs
    2. Long antenna
    3. Look like a leaf
