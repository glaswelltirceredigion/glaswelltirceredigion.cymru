## 1. Quiz
- Pwy ydw i? Who am I? I'm a butterfly/moth
- 20 questions in each quiz
- 5 plant groups & 5 invertebrate groups, 2 photos in each = 20 questions
- For purpose of quiz, children don't need to know difference between species in group
## 2. Workshop
- Laminated workshop sheet
- Each group has 3 identifying physical features
- Taught in the workshop
- Also use guides/book
- Workshop photos can't be used in quiz
- Second workshop on school grounds
